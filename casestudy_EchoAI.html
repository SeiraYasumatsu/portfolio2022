SUMMARY

In a world where emotional support isn’t always available when it’s needed most, EchoAI offers a “digital mirror”—an AI companion that can recognize microexpressions, tone, and language patterns to reflect a user’s inner state with sensitivity and accuracy. For premium users, it seamlessly connects them with trained human coaches, creating a hybrid model that balances the scalability of AI with the irreplaceable value of human connection.

As Principal Designer, I collaborated with a multidisciplinary team of 20–30 professionals to shape the product’s vision and user experience. My contributions included defining the emotional flow of interactions, crafting intuitive and trustworthy interfaces, and ensuring every design decision reinforced a sense of empathy, safety, and connection. The result is a platform that empowers individuals to navigate anxiety, burnout, and daily challenges with greater self-awareness, resilience, and support.

PROBLEM

In moments of emotional crisis, people often suffer alone. Traditional support systems can be hard to reach, and existing digital tools fall short of offering the empathy and immediacy people truly need.

•  Emotional crises often occur at times when traditional support—friends, family, or professionals—is unavailable

•  Individuals in distress may feel isolated or reluctant to burden others with their struggles

•  Conventional mental health services are not always accessible, especially outside of regular hours

•  Many people mask their emotions, making it difficult for others to recognize when they need help

•  Current digital solutions lack the emotional intelligence to offer genuine, empathetic support

SOLUTION

How can we provide immediate, genuine emotional support to people in their darkest moments when traditional support systems are unavailable or out of reach?

EchoAI is an empathetic digital companion that uses advanced emotional AI to analyze facial microexpressions, language, and tone of voice providing 24/7 understanding and support through meaningful conversations when people need it most.
