Hi, I'm Seira.

I started out as an educator, where I learned the power of listening deeply and designing with empathy. That mindset still guides me today as a designer—creating tools that feel intuitive, human, and meaningful.

(also a tinkerer and dog person!)

Currently, as Head of Design at TeachMeMedical, I design patient-centered tools that make medical data clear and accessible for everyone.

I also run Cozy Corner Design, a bilingual studio that bridges English and Japanese audiences. By blending design with cultural translation, I help businesses expand their reach and build trust across markets.

My goal is simple: to design experiences that don’t just look good, but move people—and move businesses forward.
