Hi, I'm Seira.

I’m a Product Designer based in San Francisco

(also a tinkerer and educator!).

With 2+ years of product design experience, I aim to create simple and elegant products based on actual human experiences, having done so at Bloc in which I designed a collaborative family mobile app to improve communication between busy families.

Working with children as a creative director piqued my curiosity and interest in product design, leading to an amazing collaboration with 60+ people, showcasing a project to over 100 people, and winning an award for the Stanford Design challenge to promote longevity for all ages.

My passion for children and education has taken me on a remarkable journey from educator to a designer that understands both worlds, and I am eager to continue aligning systems and the real world through creative design solutions. Let’s tinker together!
